Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that features layers of wide pasta sheets, rich meat sauce,
creamy bechamel sauce, and plenty of melted cheese. It's a beloved comfort food that's
both hearty and delicious. Here's a basic recipe for making lasagna:

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef or a mixture of ground beef and pork
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

for the Bachamel Sauce

  • 4 cups (1 liter) whole milk
  • 1/2 cup (60g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup (113g) unsalted butter
  • A pinch of nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Other Ingredients

  • 12-16 lasagna noodles, cooked al dente
  • 2 cups (200g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup (100g) grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:


1.Prepare the Meat Sauce

  1. In a large saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and garlic and sauté until they become translucent
  3. Add the ground meat and cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste.
  5. Season with oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  6. Simmer the sauce for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

2.Prepare the Bachamel Sauce

  1. n a separate saucepan, melt the butter over low heat.
  2. Add the flour and whisk continuously to create a smooth roux. Cook for a couple of minutes, but do not let it brown.
  3. Gradually whisk in the milk to create a smooth sauce.
  4. Add a pinch of nutmeg and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Continue to cook and whisk until the sauce thickens, about 10-15 minutes.

3.Assemble the Lasagna

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a 9x13-inch (23x33 cm) baking dish, start by spreading a small amount of meat sauce on the bottom.
  3. Layer with cooked lasagna noodles.
  4. Add a layer of meat sauce, followed by bechamel sauce, and a sprinkling of both mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Repeat the layers until you've used all the ingredients, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and plenty of cheese on top.

4.Bake

  1. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes.
  2. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.

5.Serve

  1. Allow the lasagna to rest for a few minutes before serving. This helps it set and makes it easier to slice.
  2. Serve your delicious homemade lasagna with a side of garlic bread or a fresh green salad.
