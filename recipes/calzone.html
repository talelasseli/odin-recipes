Calzone

Description

A calzone is a type of Italian oven-baked turnover that resembles a folded pizza or a
tuffed pizza. It is made from pizza dough and typically filled with a variety of ingredients

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Filling

    like cheese etc...

  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough
  2. Add the Filling
  3. Fold and Seal
  4. Bake
  5. Serve
